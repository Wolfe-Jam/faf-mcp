Every account gets 10,000 free tokens per month. This resets on the 1st of each month. No credit card required.

What happens when I run out of tokens?

You'll see a low balance warning at 1,000 tokens. When you hit zero, you can top up instantly via Stripe (minimum $10).

No! Purchased tokens never expire. Top up once, use them whenever.
